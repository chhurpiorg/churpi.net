Recipes

Ways Churpi appears at the table.

These are educational serving ideas, not strict regional recipes. Local preparations vary by household, valley, and available ingredients.

Filter

Choose a style.

Churpi achar idea

Combine soft Churpi with tomato, chili, herbs, and a little oil for a tangy side.

Greens and Churpi curry

Cook leafy greens with onion, garlic, spices, and crumbled soft Churpi.

Trail chew

Carry a small piece of hard Churpi and soften it slowly. Avoid biting hard pieces forcefully.

Potato Churpi stew

Simmer potatoes, tomato, green chili, and soft Churpi for a warming mountain-style dish.

Eating note

Hard Churpi can be extremely firm. Chew gradually, let it soften, and avoid giving large hard pieces to small children or anyone with dental concerns.
