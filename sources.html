Glossary

Search key terms.

Yak milk

Milk from yaks, important in high-altitude pastoral regions.

Chauri

A yak-cattle hybrid often associated with Himalayan dairy systems.

Whey

The liquid separated from curds during cheesemaking.

Hard Churpi

A dried form of Churpi known for extreme firmness and long storage.

Soft Churpi

A fresh or less-dried form commonly used in cooked dishes.
